Challenge Document (Player-Facing)
Title: The Oracle's Blind Spot
Category: Pwn / Binary Exploitation
Difficulty: Hard

Briefing:
You have acquired the Sector Oracle Calibration Tool. The engineers boast that it is completely memory-safe because they rigorously check that users cannot write past the end of the diagnostic array.

However, they seem to have forgotten a fundamental rule of mathematics and data types. Find the blind spot, overwrite your security clearance, and access the mainframe.

Rules:

Run the binary locally on a Linux machine.

To unlock the flag, your security clearance must equal 0x2152414a.
