📖 1. Mission Briefing
The world of Minecraft lost one of its greatest legends, a tactician who proved that "Technoblade never dies." To solve this challenge, you must navigate through a digital trail to find the specific timestamps of his life’s journey.

The Objective: Find the Month and Year of the legend's birth and the Month and Year of his passing.

Flag Format: Flag{Month_Year_Month_Year}

🔍 2. Investigation (Step-by-Step)
Step 1: The Initial Pivot
The challenge begins with an image of a player watching a Minecraft tribute. Upon closer inspection (zooming into the Address Bar of the browser in the image), players identify the custom YouTube handle:

youtube.com/@TheGreatPotatoKingShorts

Step 2: Analyzing the YouTube Channel
Visiting the channel reveals a dedicated tribute space.

The Banner: Contains the text "Start and end of an era." This is a direct hint that the flag involves the start (birth) and end (death) of the person the channel is dedicated to.

The Content: The channel features clips of Technoblade, identifying the subject of our investigation.

Step 3: Open Source Research
Using the identity "Technoblade," we perform a search for his biographical information.

Search Query: Technoblade birth and death dates or Technoblade Wikipedia.

Finding 1 (Birth): Multiple sources (Wikipedia, Fandom) confirm Alexander (Technoblade) was born on June 1, 1999.

Finding 2 (Death): His family released the "so long nerds" video on June 30, 2022. Sources confirm he passed away in June 2022.

Step 4: Constructing the Flag
Following the required format Flag{Month_Year_Month_Year}:

Start: June_1999

End: June_2022

🏆 3. Final Flag
Flag{June_1999_June_2022}
