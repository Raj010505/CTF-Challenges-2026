This write-up outlines the steps to solve the OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) challenge involving the CSESA website and social media footprint.

Challenge Write-up: The Presidential Trail
Challenge Overview
Target: CSESA (Computer Science Engineering Students Association)

Goal: Find the hidden flag located in an ex-president's Instagram post description.

Entry Point: csesa.live

Step 1: Reconnaissance (The Website)
The journey begins at the official CSESA website. Navigating to csesa.live, the objective is to find links to their social media presence.

Action: Scroll to the footer or the "Contact" section.

Discovery: Locate the official Instagram icon link which directs to the CSESA official handle.

Step 2: Identifying the Target (The Ex-President)
To find the specific post mentioned, we need to identify the former leadership.

Action: Navigate to the "Team" or "About Us" section on the website.

Analysis: Look for the "Hall of Fame" or "Past Committees." Identify Divesh Kankani, who served as the President of the association.

Step 3: Social Media Deep-Dive
Once the name is confirmed, the search shifts to Instagram to find his personal or professional profile.

Action: Search for "Divesh Kankani" on Instagram or find him tagged in the "Handover" posts on the official @csesa_ltce (or similar) account.

Target Profile: Access Divesh’s profile.

Step 4: Locating the Flag
The challenge specifies that the flag is in the description of one of his posts.

Action: Browse through posts related to his time at CSESA, specifically "Year-end" wrap-ups, "Core Committee" announcements, or technical posts.

Extraction: Open the post and read the caption carefully. The flag is typically formatted as CSESA{...}.
